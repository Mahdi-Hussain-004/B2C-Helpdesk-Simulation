# Canned Responses (Macro Library)

This library ensures rapid, empathetic, and legally compliant communication with end-users.

### 1. Billing - Refund Request Acknowledgment
Hi [Customer Name],
Thank you for reaching out to our Support Team. I understand you are requesting a refund for a recent transaction, and I apologize for any frustration this has caused. To help me locate this charge and process your request immediately, could you please reply to this email with the **Transaction ID** or a screenshot of the receipt? As soon as we verify the transaction against your Account ID, we will get this sorted out for you.

### 2. Trust & Safety - Account Suspension Appeal
Hello [Customer Name],
We have received your appeal regarding the recent suspension of your account. Our automated systems flagged your account for a potential violation of our Terms of Service. I have officially escalated your ticket to our Trust & Safety Moderation Team for a manual review. Please note that this review process can take up to 48 hours. Thank you for your patience.

### 3. Tech - Bug/Crash Acknowledgment
Hi [Customer Name],
Thank you for taking the time to report this issue! We are sorry to hear the app is crashing on you. I have logged this behavior and forwarded it to our Engineering Team. To help them isolate the bug, could you please reply with your current Device Model and OS version? Thank you for helping us improve the platform!
