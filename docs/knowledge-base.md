# Knowledge Base (Ticket Deflection Articles)

### Article 1: How to Manage or Cancel Your Premium Subscription
We make it easy to control your subscription directly from your device. Please note that uninstalling the app will not automatically cancel your recurring payments.

**For iOS (Apple) Users:**
1. Open the Settings app on your device.
2. Tap your Name/Apple ID at the top.
3. Tap Subscriptions.
4. Select our app and tap Cancel Subscription.

**For Android (Google Play) Users:**
1. Open the Google Play Store app.
2. Tap your Profile Icon.
3. Tap Payments & subscriptions, then Subscriptions.
4. Select our app and tap Cancel subscription.

### Article 2: Troubleshooting: What to do if the app keeps crashing
If you are experiencing unexpected crashes, 90% of these issues can be resolved with a few quick steps:
* **Step 1: Force Close and Restart:** Swipe the app away completely from your multitasking view, then reopen it.
* **Step 2: Check for Updates:** Ensure you have the latest version downloaded from the App Store or Google Play.
* **Step 3: Clear App Cache (Android):** Go to Settings > Apps > Storage > Clear Cache.
